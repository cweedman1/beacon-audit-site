How it works

A practical website review method.

Beacon checks the public website, explains what the results mean for the business, and turns the findings into a practical order of work.

01

Review

Beacon checks the public website signals that affect trust, speed, search visibility, accessibility, and maintainability.

Takeaway: You see the same health categories browsers, customers, and search engines can already notice.

02

Understand

Beacon explains what each result means in business terms, without asking owners to sort through raw tool output.

Takeaway: You can tell the difference between a real priority, a maintenance item, and a low-impact warning.

03

Prioritize

Beacon turns review results into a practical work plan ordered by impact, effort, and business value.

Takeaway: You know what to fix first, why it matters, and what can wait.
